HOA Maintenance Programs

Choose a simple program, then add seasonal and improvement work as needed.

Weekly Common Area Maintenance

  • Mowing (as applicable), trimming, edging, and blowing
  • Walkway / curb-line cleanup
  • Spot checks for irrigation issues
  • Reporting of damage, dead spots, or safety concerns

Seasonal Cleanups

  • Spring cleanup: debris removal, bed reset, cutbacks
  • Fall cleanup: leaf removal, final trims, winter prep
  • Power raking / dethatching (as needed)

Weeding & Bed Care

  • Weeding cycles for beds and hard edges
  • Mulch / rock refresh and top-offs
  • Pruning for sight-lines and appearance

Irrigation & Drainage Support

  • Sprinkler maintenance / repair
  • Spigot installs, downspout cleaning
  • Gutter cleaning and gutter grate installs
